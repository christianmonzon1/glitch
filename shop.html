Rev up your career in the automotive industry with our comprehensive training program. Robotics & Drones Engineering

The focus is on students learning basic robot building instructions, programming, and movement, then moving on to working with sensors and more complex robot behaviors. Projects cover key STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) concepts. One of our most popular CTE classes, Students learn leadership skills and skill in robotics language and creation. Start your journey in robotics and engineering here at Edison!
