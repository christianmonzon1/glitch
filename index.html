About Us

At Thomas A. Edsion Career & Technical High School, innovation meets opportunity and work to make sure our students have the oppurtinties to form a better tommorow! As an educational institution dedicated to Career and Technical Education, our main mission is shaping the workforce of tomorrow, and it all starts with the admission to Edision. Our curriculum blends academic excellence with hands-on learning experiences through our multipule shop and CTE based classes, providing students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in a wide range of careers to become the people of tommorow.

How is Edision Different?

With a focus on innovation and adaptability, we create enviroments of critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills essential for thriving in today's evolving job market. Our graduates emerge not only with a solid academic foundation but also with the confidence and with the expertises to pursue their dreams and make a lasting impact in their chosen fields.
